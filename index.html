Our topic is about remote learning during Covid-19. We primarily focus on the education situation for children in New York City.



Given that access to education and green space are important social determinants for children's health, there are concerns that their health is being compromised by remote learning which now many of NYC's children are forced to go through under Covid-19.



CDC study finds that Coronavirus kills far more Hispanic and Black children than White youths.



Therefore children of color who come from low-income households, have chosen to do online learning.
